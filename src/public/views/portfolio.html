How I Got Started

Originally from Hamilton, Ontario. I've been living in California for the last 4 years. Always been obessed with food, environment, science, restaurants and books, I decided to take chemical engineering and enviromental science to learn more about the world.

In Canada, I worked as a food inspector for OMAFRA and found my passion for quality and interaction with farmers, business owners and workers. This gave me a wonderful foundation on teamwork, diligence and disclipine.

But I wanted to challenge myself, so I decided to pack my bags and head down to LA. Not expecting to stay long, I've re-discovered my passion for food, enviroment and solving challenges for clients.
